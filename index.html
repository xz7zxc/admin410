History

We started this store in 2016, by Zhou Xucheng. We only sold dog foods at the beginning before we were getting so many customers around Durham. Then after so many years, we reach what we are now.

Why use us

We have everything you need to take care of your pets, including food, treats, toys, litter, supplies, medicine, chicken coops, rabbit hutches, dog houses, cages and Wild Bird Seed! We also work with the local animal shelters and rescues to help adopt out animals.
